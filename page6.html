A New Horror Story

The Red Plague is a total-conversion Subnautica mod that transforms the game into a darker, more threatening experience. A new infection — the “Red Plague” — mutates lifeforms in unpredictable ways.

The mod introduces new story chapters, characters, logs, and environmental hazards that push survival to extreme levels.
