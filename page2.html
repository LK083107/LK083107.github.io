Diverse Environments

Planet 4546B features a wide range of biomes, each with unique flora, fauna, and resources. From the peaceful Safe Shallows to the hostile Lava Lakes, every area presents different challenges.

Major Biomes

  • Safe Shallows — starting area, bright and resource-rich.
  • Kelp Forest — home to Stalkers and Creepvine clusters.
  • Grand Reef — deep, mysterious, filled with floating anchors.
  • Lost River — acidic waters and precursor remains.
  • Lava Zones — end-game biome inhabited by Sea Dragon Leviathans.

Key Locations

The Aurora wreck, precursor facilities, Degasi bases, and hidden caves offer essential story progression and upgrades.
