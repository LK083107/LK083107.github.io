How to Install

  1. Download QModManager, SML Helper (required mod loaders) or Vortex (Mod manager).
  2. Visit the Red Plague page on Nexus Mods.
  3. Extract files to your Subnautica “QMods” or “Mods” folder. Or download directly onto Vortex by hitting 'Vortex' button instead of manual download.
  4. Download dependencies (Things the mod requires in order to properly function).
  5. Launch the game and ensure the mod is enabled.
