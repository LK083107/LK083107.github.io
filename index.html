Welcome to Planet 4546B

After the crash of the Aurora spacecraft, you awake as Ryley Robinson — the sole confirmed survivor. With only your damaged Lifepod and scattered emergency supplies, you must adapt to surviving on an alien, ocean-covered world.

Subnautica is an open-world survival adventure filled with exploration, crafting, diving, and discovery. Players must manage oxygen, health, hunger, and thirst while uncovering the secrets hidden below the waves.

Your ultimate goal: survive long enough to escape the planet… if the planet lets you.
