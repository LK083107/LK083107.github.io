Crafting

The Fabricator is the heart of survival, allowing you to convert raw materials into tools, food, water, and equipment. Larger vehicles are constructed using the Mobile Vehicle Bay.

Essential Gear

  • Scanner
  • Oxygen Tanks
  • Survival Knife
  • Seaglide
  • PRAWN Suit
  • Cyclops Submarine

Efficient resource gathering — such as scanning fragments and mining sandstone and shale — is key to progression.
